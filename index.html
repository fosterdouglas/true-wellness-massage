About Us!

True wellness massage offers mobile massage therapy treatments in the comfort of your own home, located in the heart of Cochrane, Alberta. Providing you with the techniques to alleviate pain, stress, chronic conditions, musculoskeletal conditions, and many more specific aliments of the body. Whether you just want relaxation massage, or a therapeutic massage, we have a treatment for your needs. TWM offers a variety of special techniques to ensure your needs are taken care of professional, and accurately. Helping you restore your body and mind. Each session will include a physical assessment to ensure the appropriate treatments are given.
